Policy

Our Policy

Abramo Nogcci is interested in ensuring the ultimate satisfaction of her consumers.
Based on this, she has drafted policies that help grow customer's confidence and satisfaction in any dealing and improve relationship between her and her patronizers.

Return Policy

To ensure total satisfice and further prove our aim of rolling out quality, our policy of return serves as a point of furtherance.
We hereby accept any return of our product if the following occur:
(1) variation in the colour against what was intended.
(2) variation in the style against what is ordered.
(3) alteration in quality of the sole, leather and accessory.
(4) delay in delivery without prior information and agreeable terms reached.
(5) conspicuous shabby suture and detailing on the sole, leather or on the seam.
(6) unusual cum unacceptable contortion on the product.
(7) any physical damage caused during production.

Howbeit, these conditions must be satisfactorily proven by both sides to be without any shadow of doubt. This return policy covers 10 days after delivery to customer.

Payment Policy

Our customer payment policy is based on earnest consideration at the end of the customer.
At this end, we accept 50 percent up front payment for production to commence fully .When production is completed and proven so, the customer is expected to make full remittance for delivery at the designated port.

Delivery Policy

Our delivery destination is subject to the customer's wish. If by Air cargo, we deliver to the designated cargo Airport. If by Shipment, we deliver to the designated Harbour with the customer bearing every cost for shipment and every necessary custom duties as required by the respective Governments.
